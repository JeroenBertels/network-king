Gameplay Guide

How Network King works

The goal

Meet each fictional character in order, save useful notes, and unlock the next level until you finish the full trail.

For networkers

  • Open your event and start with the first unlocked level.
  • Scan a badge QR code to open that character.
  • Write down your takeaway to complete that level.
  • Once a note is saved, the next level unlocks automatically.

For admins

  • Create events, networkers, and characters.
  • Download printable badge PDFs with QR codes.
  • Track what networkers noted for each character.
  • Use CSV import and export to manage larger lists quickly.

What everyone sees

  • Public visitors can browse events and see overall progress.
  • Networkers only reveal characters they have unlocked.
  • The leaderboard ranks players by completed levels.
